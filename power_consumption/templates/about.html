About the Project

Project Abstract

Accurate forecasting of power consumption is essential for efficient energy management, cost reduction, and enhancing grid stability. This study presents a deep learning-based approach using the Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) algorithm to predict energy consumption patterns based on historical power usage data.


The system processes multiple electrical parameters including Voltage, Intensity, and Reactive Power to forecast the Global Active Power effectively. Experimental results demonstrate that the proposed LSTM-based model outperforms traditional statistical methods, making it a reliable tool for smart energy management and load forecasting.
